Modelling a Zombie Apocolaypse with SIR

Would humans survive a Zombie Apocolaypse? To answer that question, data scientists often refer to the SIR disease model. The model is simple - there are 3 types of actors is on our post-apocolayptic world: Humans, Zombies, and the Dean. (Originally labelled "S", "I", and "R", our model is more appropriately abbreviated as the HZD model). In the model, the drama plays out pretty simply. If a Human meets a Zombie, they become a Zombie. After a certain amount of time, Zombies die of their own accord. Whether the human race lives or dies depends on how frequently Zombies meet Humans, and how frequently Zombies die. Change these parameters below to see if you'll survive!
